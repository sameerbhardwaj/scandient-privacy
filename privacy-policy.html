Summary: Scandient processes food label images in real-time to detect ingredients but does not store images, collect personal information, or track users in any way. Your privacy is fully protected.

Introduction

Scandient ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Images You Scan

  • When you scan a food label, the image is temporarily sent to our servers for processing
  • Images are processed to extract ingredient text using Optical Character Recognition (OCR)
  • Images are NOT stored permanently - they are deleted immediately after processing
  • We do not retain, save, or use your images for any other purpose

Data We Process

  • Ingredient text extracted from scanned images
  • Detection results (vegan, vegetarian, or contains meat)
  • This data is processed in real-time and not stored long-term

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles

How We Use Your Information

We use the images you scan solely to:

  1. Extract ingredient text using OCR technology
  2. Analyze ingredients to detect meat, dairy, eggs, honey, and other animal products
  3. Provide you with instant results about whether food is vegan, vegetarian, or contains meat

Data Storage and Security

  • Images: Processed in real-time and immediately deleted
  • Network Communication: All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption
  • Third-Party Services: We use Railway.app for hosting our backend infrastructure
  • No Long-Term Storage: We do not maintain databases of user scans or images

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Railway.app: Cloud hosting for our backend API
  • Tesseract OCR: Open-source OCR engine for text extraction

These services do not have access to your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete. Each scan is processed independently with no connection to you as a user.

Data Retention

  • Scanned Images: Deleted immediately after processing (within seconds)
  • Processing Results: Not stored or associated with any user
  • Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics or statistics

International Data Transfers

Our backend service is hosted on Railway.app, which may process data in various locations. All data transfers are secured using industry-standard encryption.
