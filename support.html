Frequently Asked Questions

How does Scandient work?

Simply take a photo of any food label's ingredients list. Scandient uses advanced OCR (text recognition) to read the ingredients and instantly tells you if the product is vegan, vegetarian, or contains meat.

What ingredients does Scandient detect?

Scandient detects:

  • Meat and poultry ingredients
  • Dairy products (milk, cheese, whey, etc.)
  • Eggs and egg derivatives
  • Honey and bee products
  • Other animal-derived ingredients

Is my data safe?

Absolutely! Your images are processed in real-time and deleted immediately after analysis. We don't store your photos or track your activity. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The scan didn't work correctly. What should I do?

For best results:

  • Ensure good lighting
  • Hold your phone steady
  • Make sure the ingredients list is clearly visible and in focus
  • Avoid glare or shadows on the label

If you continue to have issues, please contact us at intel.bytes.ai@gmail.com

Does Scandient work offline?

No, Scandient requires an internet connection to process images and analyze ingredients using our cloud-based OCR and detection system.

Is Scandient free?

Yes! Scandient is completely free to use with no ads or in-app purchases.

I found an error in the detection. How can I report it?

We're constantly improving our detection accuracy. Please email us at intel.bytes.ai@gmail.com with:

  • A photo of the ingredients list
  • What Scandient detected
  • What the correct result should be
